Introduction

He lived and studied in his home town Chas, Bokaro SteelCity. As a young Boy he was always inspired by the stories of the great patroits of the Nation and dreamt of working for the progress of motherland. He was especially inspired and influenced by the nationalist Spirit of Atal bihar Vajpayee jee.
